Muscles, Myokine, Mytochondrien


Very important for the body and energy production are: MUSCLES,MYOKINESand MYOCHONDRIA.
Muscles are the largest metabolic organ. Muscles and the brain utilize the sugar we consume. When muscles are active, they release myokines. These are enzyme-like messengers that stimulate the body to grow and the like, especially the metabolic process. The mitochondria are the little "furnaces" in us. The mitochondria form a network that extends throughout the entire cell. They provide the energy that the cells need. It is therefore important that we have enough muscle mass. Watch details:
