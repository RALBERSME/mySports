Do you want to have many problems or just one?




Most people dream of a happy life. People who are sick have only one wish (problem):
"I want to get healthy!"

When we are healthy, we can solve and implement many wishes (problems).
Physical activity is good for our health. So why don't we use this opportunity to keep ourselves fit, flexible and healthy? Physical activity can also be fun. Of course, you shouldn't overdo it with sport. Sport also helps reduce stress.
