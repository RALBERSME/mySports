Welcome to Old Egypt!


Maybe you didn't know it, but in ancient Egypt there were many temples along the Nile. They were dedicated to different deities and were among other things used for tests of courage. You can go into one of these temples right now; it is a special invitation that only selected people receive. Your task is to dive into the depths and find the way out of the labyrinth. The water is very murky, means you can only see a few centimetres directly in front of you. It is therefore important to make quick decisions, because your lungs need oxygen again after 2 minutes. If you don't manage to surface within 2 minutes, you die. But if you find the exit from the labyrinth within 2 minutes, you will be celebrated.
If you cannot swim you might go back to the start page and choose "aquatics"; then come back to this temple.
